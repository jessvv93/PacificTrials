The Yurts at Pacific Trials

What is a Yurt?

Our luxury Yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor. and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers or clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
